 - To accommodate for the above, a certain amount of HP and PP can be restored for every Pokémon in your party after every battle by talking to Nurse Joy once buffs have been unlocked

  - PP Recovery Lv. 1-3: Increase PP restored by Nurse Joy
  - Status Recovery: Nurse Joy heals status conditions. Costs 3 energy
  - Status Immunity: Your Pokémon are immune to status conditions. Costs 4 energy
  - Interference Immunity: Your Pokémon are immune to flinching, being trapped or confused. Costs 3 energy
  - Highest Vigilance: Your Pokémon won't receive critical hits for 999 turns (effectively the whole battle). Costs 3 energy
